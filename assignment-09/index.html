How to Play Hangman

Materials

  • Pen and Paper
  • 2+ Players

Directions

Hangman is a simple word guessing game. One player tries to figure out an unknown word by guessing letters. If too many letters which do not appear in the word are guessed, the player is hanged (and loses).


Setup the game by having one player draw a gallow and a blank underline for each letter in the unknown word. As correct letters in the word are guessed, write them above the correct underline.


If a letter not in the word is guess, draw a picture of a person on the gallow. Only draw one body part for each incorrect letter guess. Usually, the person is drawn in 6 parts in the order: head, body, left leg, right leg, left arm, right arm. The objective is to guess the correct word before the player gets "hung"
